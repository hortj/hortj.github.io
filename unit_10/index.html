ABOUT



Cole Horton is an author, historian, and pop culture writer living in Los Angeles, California. He is a 2008 graduate from Indiana University with a degree in History. His greatest interests lie in the interwar period and the Second World War.

Cole is currently a player insights manager, working primarily on Star Wars. Previously he worked in consumer insights and brand building for the world's largest CPG company - driving strategy for billion dollar brands every day. He specialized in digital, social media, and retail marketing.

Cole is co-author of the upcoming book Star Wars®: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know from DK Publishing.

WORK



Cole's first book that he co-authored, Star Wars®: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know from DK Publishing, is a visual guide full of key facts, interesting trivia, and infographics to get you prepared for future Star Wars films. The book is part of the "Journey to Star Wars: The Force Awakens" and comes out September 4, 2015.

Cole writes a monthly feature for StarWars.com, known as 'From World War to Star Wars.' He is also a contributing Historian to Marvel.com, Marvel's Augmented Reality App, and is a geeky runner for runDisney.
